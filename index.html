What is this website and how do I use it?

This is a website with data on over 5,000 exoplanets. By clicking on discover, you can search for an exoplanet or filter for specific criteria. Clicking on an exoplanet will allow you to explore that exoplanet's information, what would be needed to live there, and what the effect of living there would be on the human body. It is worth noting that you will be able to see what would be needed to live on the planet and how that would affect the body even if the planet is not habitable. This is because man-made habitats could allow someone to live on an inhabitable planet, but the planet would not be able to support liquid water. All information is from NASA's Exoplanet Archive.
