Welcome to Exopossibilities

What is this website and how do I use it?

This is a website with data on over 5,000 exoplanets. By clicking on discover, you can search for an exoplanet or filter for specific criteria. Clicking on an exoplanet will allow you to explore that exoplanet's information, what would be needed to live there, and what the effect of living there would be on the human body.
